One-liner

The crew companion for AIMS eCrew pilots and cabin crew. Roster, logbook, fatigue tracker, layover map, and opt-in roster sharing with your colleagues — all on your iPhone, all private.

The app

Flyers is a native iOS 26 app that wraps the AIMS eCrew web portal — the scheduling system used by ~150 airlines worldwide — in a fast, offline-first, privacy-first interface.

Currently shipping for Etihad Airways pilots. The architecture supports adding new airlines as a config-only change once each tenant's portal is verified against live data.

Core features

  • Roster — month view with block / duty / off / flight-day totals, date-range filter, jump-to-today.
  • Flight detail — crew listing with heavy-crew pairing variations (A/B/C/D), great-circle route map, registration, aircraft type, load, layover hotel.
  • Logbook — auto-built 12-month flight history with Aerolog-compatible export.
  • Stats & FTL — block, duty, and flight-time-limit totals across rolling windows.
  • Expiry tracking — licences, medicals, recurrent training countdown.
  • Fatigue tracker — daily sleep log with 7-day chart.
  • Layover & airport info — local time, weather, sunrise/sunset, hotel notes.
  • Crew Mail — read-only mirror of your eCrew inbox.
  • Live Activities — next-duty countdown on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island.

Flyers Social (new)

Cross-airline opt-in roster sharing between Flyers users. Add friends by their public @handle, accept or block as you choose, and hide individual events from anyone you don't want to share with. End-to-end via Apple's CloudKit — no third-party server, no analytics, no telemetry.

Flyers Pro — $2.99/month or $19.99/year (7-day free trial) — unlocks unlimited friends, push updates when their roster changes, and per-event hiding.

Privacy posture

Flyers is built around a single principle: the Developer cannot read your data.

  • Credentials are stored only in the iOS Keychain on the user's device.
  • Roster and logbook data are cached locally via SwiftData.
  • Social features run on Apple's CloudKit; the Developer operates no server.
  • No analytics, no third-party SDKs, no telemetry, no advertising.

Full details: Privacy Policy.

Pricing

  • Free tier: all core features (roster, logbook, stats, fatigue, layover info, crew mail) plus read-only Flyers Social (claim a handle, accept up to 2 friend requests, view friends' shared rosters).
  • Flyers Pro: $2.99/month or $19.99/year (7-day free trial). Unlocks sending unlimited friend requests, publishing your roster, push updates, and per-event hiding.

Technical

  • Native iOS 26+, Swift 6 strict concurrency, SwiftUI, Liquid Glass design system.
  • CloudKit for the Social subsystem; StoreKit 2 for subscriptions.
  • No third-party dependencies beyond Apple SDKs.
  • Source: closed; verification: pre-launch sandbox testing on real devices.
